Lamb Tagine Recipe

Succulent Lamb Tagine served with Couscous

Heartwarming meal perfect for family gatherings in the evening.

Slow cooked lamb and stew along with flavour intense couscous.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs of Lamb Meat
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Spices; Paprika, Cinammon, Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Coriander, Cumin, Cardamon, Ginger, Turmeric, Cayenne Pepper, Cloves
  • 2 medium Onions
  • 5 Carrots
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 can of Chicken Broth
  • tbs of tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 1 tbsp of cornstarch (optional)
  • 1 tbsp of water (optional)

Steps:

  1. Place lamb and 2 tbsps of olive oil in a large bowl and toss to coat.
  2. Mix spices together in a large resealable bag. Add the lamb to the bag and toss to coat well. Leave regrigerated overnight.
  3. Heat remaining olive oil in a large heavy bottomed pot and add 1/3 of the lamb and cook for 5-7 minutes. Remove to a plate and cook the other batches.
  4. Add onions and carrots to the pot for 5 minutes. Stir in the garlic and ginger; continue cooking for 5 more minutes. Return the lamb to the pot and stir in lemon zest, chicken broth, tomato paste, and honey. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer, stirring occassionally, until meat is tender, 1.5 to 2 hours.
  5. If the consistency of tagine is too thin, you may thicken it with cornstarch and water slurry during the last 5 minutes.
  6. Serve over the couscous
